Chapter 3 – Plan Your Dream Birthday Party!

My Notes

Your Challenge: ₹5,000 Birthday Party for 10 Guests!

You have been given ₹5,000 by your parents to organize a unique birthday party for 10 guests at an outdoor café or restaurant.

You must plan and manage:

  • Venue booking
  • Food & drinks
  • Decorations
  • Entertainment
  • Return gifts

Goal: Create a fun, memorable, and creative party — all within budget!

Mission: Show creativity, responsibility, and smart money management!

Step-by-Step Party Plan

1. Venue Booking

Choose a cozy café or restaurant with a nice outdoor area. Negotiate a budget package within ₹2,000–₹2,500 for food and drinks.

2. Food & Drinks

Opt for a set menu or group platter. Request a simple but beautiful birthday cake within ₹1,000.

3. Unique Themes & Decorations

Pick a fun theme and use DIY or low-cost decorations for ₹500.

4. Entertainment & Activities

Plan free games, bring a Bluetooth speaker, and set up a DIY photo booth.

5. Return Gifts

Give simple, thoughtful tokens for ₹500.
