What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a class of secure digital currencies that allow direct peer-to-peer transactions without central control, enabled by cryptographic techniques and blockchain ledgers.

Key Features: Decentralized, Secure & Transparent, Peer-to-Peer, Powered by Blockchain & Cryptography.

What is Currency?

Currency is the official money issued by a government, such as Indian Rupees, US Dollars, Euros, etc. It comes in coins and paper notes with different values.
